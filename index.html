Annual Inter-IIT sports meet

Passion meets purpose at the Annual Inter-IIT Sports Meet , which is the grandest stage to showcase your sporting talents. The pride of representing your institute at such a stage is beyond words, it’s a mix of a extravagant experiences and emotions. It takes place in December every year and the Aquatics meet being hosted in October.

Inter Hostel General Championship (GC)

Hostel sentiments and rivalries come to the surface once the battle for the Inter Hostel General Championship (GC) begins. You will be well-versed with the glorious past of your respective hostels in your initial days itself when the hostel culture is introduced to you. The neck to neck competition and endless cheering for your hostel would certainly be a treasured experience.


Sportech

BSA also organises Sportech , the four-day annual sports festival, in the month of February, where colleges from all across the country compete in various sports. Sportech not only has the conventional sports, but organises events like Rugby, Baseball, KhoKho, Mountaineering, Exhibition matches, screenings. BSA also promotes students by sending teams to various external tournaments throughout the year. BSA also handles NSO and promotes learning of sports in students.
